Do you offer returns?

Yes. 7 days on everything.

7 days?

Yes, 7 days from the date you receive it, to the date you put it in the mail to return.

How do I return an item?

Email us, we’ll get you started. Samreen@relovedapp.co.uk. After we receive your email we will send you a return note that you need to print and include with your return package.

Who pays for return shipping?

The buyer does.

What happens after I’ve returned an item?

  • 1. We check the item. Is it clean? Is it as it was when we sent it to you? If all good - then we move on to;
  • 2. We put a note in to our bank asking them to refund you, and then
  • 3. Within 10-15 days of the return, money’s back on your card. Minus our quality assurance fee, which is £6.
