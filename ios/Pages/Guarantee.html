FOR SELLERS

How do I sell a dress on Reloved?

Click on the Camera icon on the Home page.

What are best practices for selling through Reloved?

  • Create high quality pictures
  • Provide detailed descriptions
  • Set price competitively

When you take pictures, practices that help are:

  • Use white background;
  • Model the dress yourself;
  • Take multiple pictures, the more the better;
  • Ensure bright lighting;
  • 'Cut your head off' when you edit the pictures.

Do I have to pay to list my dresses on Reloved?

No.

Is there a commission?

Yes, we charge 30% commission (minimum £15) on all dresses sold through Reloved. This commission allows us to cover our costs and keep the app running smoothly at all times.

How do I find out when my dress is sold?

You will receive an email from us.

Do I have to offer returns?

Yes. All dresses sold on Reloved carry a 7 day return option. This will actually help you more than you think.

How will I get paid?

We'll send a cheque to the address listed in your contact details (access via Home page in the App).

When will I get paid?

We'll mail a cheque to you within 14 days of the sale being completed.

Can the buyer see my contact details?

At no point will the buyer see your contact details or be able to contact you directly; as a seller you always deal only with the Reloved team.

FOR BUYERS

Do you offer returns?

Yes. 7 days on everything.

7 days?

Yes, 7 days from the date you receive it, to the date you put it in the mail to return.

How do I return an item?

Email us at hello@relovedapp.co.uk. After we receive your email we will send you a return note that you need to print and include with your return package.

Who pays for return shipping?

The buyer does.

What happens after I’ve returned an item?

  • We check the item. Is it clean? Is it as it was when we sent it to you? If all good - then we move on to;
  • We put a note in to our bank asking them to refund you, and then
  • Within 10-15 days of the return, money’s back on your card. Minus our quality assurance fee, which is £6.

I just ordered a dress. How long will it take to reach me?

In most cases, 10-12 days. In exceptionally (good) cases, 3-5 days.

Why 10-12 days for delivery?

This is how it becomes 10-12 days:
First, the seller sends us the dress. Royal Mail, standard delivery, 3-5 days.
Second, we check the dress, that’s 1 day.
Third, we ship the dress freshly cleaned and square-boxed to you. Royal Mail, standard delivery, 3-5 days again. So 3-5 + 1 + 3-5 = 10-12. In the future this will be much lower.

How do you ensure all your items are genuine?

We check them, piece by piece.

What address will you send my dress to?

We ship items to the address you list in My Profile section.

I live in the UK, will you deliver to me?

Yes.

I live outside the UK will you deliver to me?

We deliver anywhere in the European Union.
